# Subscribe to Ibby Dot Blog


## News Reader

You can use a news reader or feed reader to follow updates to subscribe to my blog using [this link.](https://ibby.blog/feed)

### What is a News Reader?

A lot of blogs and websites with feeds use standard formats for feeds. A news reader app can access these feeds from multiples website.

This is an example of what a news reader app looks like:

![](/newsreader.jpg)

If you're looking for a feed reader app to use these are some popular options:

* [Newsblur](https://newsblur.com/) (web; Android; iOS)
* [Aggregator](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.tughi.aggregator) (Android)
* [Liferea](https://lzone.de/liferea/) (Linux)
* [Vienna](https://www.vienna-rss.com/) (macOS)
* [Newsflow] (Windows)
